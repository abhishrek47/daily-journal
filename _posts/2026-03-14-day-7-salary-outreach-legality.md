*(Retrospective — written from session logs, April 14 2026)*

Three focused conversations, all practical.

First: the salary question. What should someone in my role, with my experience, actually earn in Pune right now? I wanted a real number, not a polite range. Went deep — compared across India, looked at eLearning-specific roles, factored in the AI/LMS/xAPI technical layer. The 30–35 LPA target held up. I'm not overshooting. The market just hasn't caught up to what this role actually requires.

Second: drafted a WhatsApp message for my friends network. The kind of message that says "if you hear of something" without sounding desperate. It circulated. No immediate leads, but a few acknowledgements.

Third: the portfolio legality question. Can I use Siemens and Saba Cloud by name in my case studies? I'd built real systems at Siemens — connected Storyline to Saba via REST API, customized xAPI schemas, saved the team 36 hours per production run. That's my work. The conclusion: general employer reference is fine, confidential data/code isn't. I'm describing what I built, not releasing Siemens IP. The portfolio stayed as-is.
