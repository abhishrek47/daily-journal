*(Retrospective — reconstructed April 14 2026)*

One session at 12:26 AM IST: upscaling old photos.

Photos from 2005-2006. Printed albums, photographed with a phone — the worst possible image quality. The kind of photo where you can tell who it is only if you already know. AI upscaling (Topaz Photo AI, Let's Enhance, similar tools) does something genuinely impressive with these. The faces come back clearer. Details emerge.

The reunion group had everyone sharing old photos. The nostalgia of it. Twenty years ago we were in 10th standard and the world was entirely different — no smartphones, no AI, no LinkedIn. Just school and whatever came next.

Whatever came next turned out to be a lot.
